Collectible Teddy Panda

Meet Chen, an adorable bamboo dweller inspired by traditional Chinese style!

* Height: 12 cm (9 cm sitting)
* Made from two-tone hand-dyed vintage plush
* Filled with cedar sawdust, cedar wood wool, and metal granules for a pleasant weight and firm stuffing
* Hand-sewn glass eyes
* Fully jointed head and paws (5 cotter pin joints)
* Cannot stand unaided
* Decorated with an aged metal bell in a vintage style

The panda comes with a charming mini composition featuring natural bamboo and sisal reed.

Chen’s look is completed with a hand-crocheted jute hat embroidered with the Chinese character for “Good Fortune” (吉). The hat is adorned with a red ribbon, one of China’s most recognizable symbols of protection, vitality, and prosperity. The braided detail on the straw hat is a classic element of traditional Chinese-inspired design. While it once had historical associations, today it is appreciated primarily as an aesthetic and theatrical feature of Chinese cultural style.

A unique collectible created for lovers of Teddy bears and artist-made animals.

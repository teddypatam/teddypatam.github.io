Artist teddy monkey “Teddy Steve”, handmade in classic teddy technique.
Height 20 cm (15 cm sitting). Made of German mohair; palms and soles from mini-stoff. Stuffed with cedar wood shavings, cedar wood wool and metal granules for a pleasant weight. German glass eyes, oil paint toning. Fully jointed head and limbs with cotter pin joints; head mounted on double O-rings for enhanced mobility. Collectible interior piece, not intended for children.
